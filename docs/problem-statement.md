# AI Interview Copilot - Problem Statement

## Overview

Hiring and technical screening are time-consuming, expensive, and often inconsistent processes. Recruiters and hiring managers spend significant effort reviewing resumes, conducting interviews, evaluating candidate responses, and generating feedback reports.

Many organizations receive hundreds or thousands of applications for a single role, making it difficult to provide a consistent and scalable interview experience.

## Problem

Traditional interviews suffer from several challenges:

* High interviewer workload.
* Inconsistent evaluation standards.
* Time-consuming candidate screening.
* Delayed feedback generation.
* Difficulty assessing large numbers of candidates efficiently.

## Proposed Solution

AI Interview Copilot is an AI-powered interview platform that automates the initial screening and evaluation process.

The system allows candidates to upload resumes, participate in AI-driven interviews, answer questions through voice interaction, and receive detailed performance reports.

The platform uses Resume Analysis, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Speech-to-Text processing, and Large Language Models to generate personalized questions, evaluate responses, and create structured interview reports.

## Target Users

### Candidates

* Students
* Job seekers
* Career switchers

### Recruiters

* HR teams
* Hiring managers
* Startup founders
* Recruitment agencies

## Objectives

* Automate technical screening.
* Provide consistent interview evaluation.
* Generate personalized interview experiences.
* Reduce recruiter workload.
* Deliver detailed candidate performance reports.
* Demonstrate modern AI-powered hiring workflows.
